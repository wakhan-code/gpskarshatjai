About GPS Karshat Jai

GPS Karshat Jai is a government primary school dedicated to providing quality education to children in our community. We focus on nurturing young minds, fostering creativity, and instilling moral values. Our experienced teachers and staff are committed to creating a safe and inclusive environment where every student can thrive. At GPS Karshat Jai, we believe in the holistic development of children, encouraging them to excel academically while also engaging in extracurricular activities that promote teamwork and leadership.
